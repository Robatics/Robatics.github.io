About Us

ECKIG ~ Your Reliable Partner

ECKIG ENGINEERING is a leading industrial procurement, distribution and service company in Nigeria. Our affiliation with top manufacturers across the globe makes us stand out as a reliable partner with access to wide range of industrial and engineering spare parts to facilitate a seamless operation in all areas of your business. We started out in 2009 as a mainly engineering procurement company but had to venture into engineering service support when we observed the challenges our customers are facing in such areas as installation, commissioning, modification and instrument verification.

We have since grown into a dependable procurement and service partner with professionalism, resourcefulness, accountability and team spirit being the pillars of our strength. Our services cut across industries such as Food & Beverages, Oil & Gas, Primaries (mining, metals, cement) Power & Energy, Water & Wastewater, Life Sciences, Pulp & Paper and even Renewable Energy industries.

Our Vision
